Applications Open: DRAPAC24 Digital Rights Fellowship

EngageMedia is now accepting applications for the DRAPAC24 Digital Rights Fellowship, supporting participation in the upcoming assembly in Taipei, Taiwan, on August 18–19, 2025.

Deadline: June 21,, 2025, 23:59 Bangkok time (UTC+7)

Apply here: https://engagemedia.org/2025/drapac24-call-fellowship-application/

This opportunity offers limited travel support for selected participants, with priority given to those hosting or speaking in sessions centered on youth engagement. Participants from marginalized and underrepresented groups, as well as first-time speakers at regional convenings, are especially encouraged to apply.
