About Us IIRO

Research, Innovate, and Empower Technology

The India Internet Research Orgnaisation (IIRO) is an initiative started by a group of dedicated volunteers who understand and utilize the potential of the Internet to drive progress, innovation, and social change. IIRO members comprises of Technology enthusiasts, activists for digital rights, leaders of Internet governance, cyber security professionals, and other activists. All of them come together to work towards their common goal of building an inclusive and secure digital ecosystem in India. The aim of IIRO is to seek perform relevant research, policy advocacy, and community mobilization focused on shaping the future of the internet in India. Our priority areas are internet governance, cyber security, digital rights, cutting edge technologies, access and inclusion, and online safety. We work together with scholars, government officials, civil society, and business professionals in order to start conversations and propose solutions that are beneficial to the entire community. Believing in the free exchange of ideas, sharing of information, and grassroots engagement, we strive to make sure that the newest digital policies and innovations serve the public interest. As a volunteer-driven organization, we are actively willing to conduct workshops, research projects, and community discussions to provide people with the tools needed for impactful engagement in global and national Internet governance.No matter who you are - a student, researcher or policy maker - if you’re passionate about the Internet, we welcome you at IIRO. Help us enhance the future of the digital space in India by contributing, collaborating, and making an impact.
