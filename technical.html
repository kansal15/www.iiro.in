Technical World

At the India Internet Research Foundation (IIRO), technology is at the core of our research and initiatives. We believe that a strong technical foundation is essential for shaping a secure, inclusive, and innovative digital future. Our work focuses on studying and supporting key technological advancements that drive Internet infrastructure, cybersecurity, data governance, and emerging technologies.

IIRO emphasizes the technical aspects of the Internet because a well-structured, resilient, and efficient digital ecosystem is crucial for national progress. Our research spans areas such as network security, encryption protocols, cloud computing, and digital identity systems. We also explore the role of artificial intelligence, blockchain, and IoT in strengthening India’s digital capabilities. By supporting technology-driven solutions, IIRO aims to enhance digital inclusion, improve internet security, and contribute to policy frameworks that foster technological growth. Our work is dedicated to ensuring that technology remains a tool for empowerment, bridging gaps in access, and creating opportunities for a more connected and informed society.
