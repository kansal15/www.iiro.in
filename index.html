IIRO Resources

Resources

IIRO resources include research publications, policy advocacy materials, and community-driven initiatives aimed at shaping India's digital future. They focus on internet governance, cybersecurity, digital rights, and emerging technologies. Through workshops, discussions, and collaborative projects, IIRO empowers individuals with knowledge and tools for impactful engagement. As a volunteer-driven initiative, it welcomes students, researchers, and policymakers to contribute and drive positive change in the digital ecosystem.

Have Any Questions For Us?

IIRO is a dedicated research and policy think tank focused on studying the Internet ecosystem in India. We work on issues related to digital governance, cybersecurity, online privacy, and emerging internet technologies.

We focus on Internet governance and policy, Cybersecurity and digital threats, online privacy and data protection, Digital inclusion and accessibility. Emerging technologies (AI, blockchain, IoT), Freedom of expression and digital rights

IIRO welcomes researchers, policymakers, students, and professionals from diverse backgrounds, including law, technology, and public policy, who are interested in shaping India's digital future.
