Events and Fellowships

Key Research Areas
  • ICANN Fellowship

    Engages individuals in ICANN meetings to learn about DNS, policy-making, and multi-stakeholder governance. [ICANN Fellowship Program](https://www.icann.org/fellowshipprogram)

  • Next Gen Internet Governance (Next Gen IG)

    Next Gen IG is a youth-focused initiative that empowers young people to actively engage in internet governance discussions. It provides platforms for learning and leadership on key issues like digital rights, cybersecurity, and policy advocacy. Through events and workshops, participants gain skills to shape the future of the internet and influence global digital policy. For more details, visit [Next Gen IG](https://www.nextgenig.org/).

  • Internet Society (ISOC) Early Career Fellowship

    Supports emerging leaders in internet policy, technology, and governance, providing mentorship and networking opportunities. [ISOC Early Career Fellowship](https://www.internetsociety.org/fellowship/early-career/)

  • APNIC Fellowship

    Focuses on capacity building for network engineers, policymakers, and researchers, offering participation in APNIC conferences. [APNIC Fellowship](https://www.apnic.net/community/participate/fellowship/)

  • RIPE NCC Fellowship

    Provides funding for individuals to attend RIPE Meetings and gain insights into internet infrastructure and policy discussions. [RIPE NCC Fellowship](https://www.ripe.net/participate/ripe/ripe-meetings/fellowships)

  • AFRINIC Fellowship

    Supports African professionals in attending AFRINIC meetings and engaging in discussions on internet governance and networking. [AFRINIC Fellowship](https://www.afrinic.net/events/fellowship)

  • LACNIC Fellowship

    Encourages participation from Latin America and the Caribbean in LACNIC meetings to enhance knowledge of internet governance. [LACNIC Fellowship](https://www.lacnic.net/participate/fellowship)

  • APrIGF Fellowship

    Provides funding for individuals to attend the Asia-Pacific Regional Internet Governance Forum (APrIGF) and participate in policy discussions. [APrIGF Fellowship](https://aprigf.asia/fellowship/)

  • EuroDIG Fellowship

    Supports young professionals in attending the European Dialogue on Internet Governance (EuroDIG). [EuroDIG Fellowship](https://www.eurodig.org/)

  • IGF Youth Ambassadors Program

    Trains and mentors young leaders to participate in the global Internet Governance Forum (IGF). [IGF Youth Ambassadors](https://www.intgovforum.org/)

  • Youth Internet Governance Forum (YIGF)

    A youth-focused initiative that educates and engages young individuals in internet governance discussions. [YIGF](https://www.yigf.asia/)

  • India School on Internet Governance (inSIG)

    A program that builds capacity in internet governance among professionals, researchers, and students in India. [inSIG](https://insig.in/)

  • Asia Pacific Internet Governance Academy (APIGA)

    A capacity-building program for youth in the Asia-Pacific region to develop leadership and policy understanding in internet governance. [APIGA](https://apiga.asia/)

  • APIGA India

    A localized initiative inspired by APIGA, focused on engaging Indian youth in internet governance learning and leadership.

  • Asia Pacific Advanced Network (APAN)

    Provides a platform for advanced research and networking across the Asia-Pacific region, with a focus on networking technologies and internet infrastructure. [APAN](https://www.apan.net/)

  • Asia Pacific Network Group (APNG) CAMP

    A program designed to engage youth in the Asia-Pacific region with technology and policy discussions to foster a deeper understanding of the internet and its governance. [APNG CAMP](https://www.apng.org/)

  • Asia Pacific Top Level Domain Association (APTLD)

    Supports the development and management of country-code top-level domains (ccTLDs) in the Asia-Pacific region, fostering collaboration on internet governance and DNS issues. [APTLD](https://www.aptld.org/)

  • NetMission Ambassador Program

    The NetMission Ambassador Program is an initiative by NetMission.Asia that aims to empower and engage youth from across the Asia-Pacific region in the field of Internet Governance. To learn more about the NetMission Ambassador Program, visit NetMission.

IG Events Happening at the National Level
  • IGF India

    IGF India is a key event for policy experts, academia, industry leaders, and civil society to engage in open discussions on internet governance. The forum promotes dialogue on critical issues like digital rights, cybersecurity, and internet access, aiming to create collaborative solutions for India’s internet ecosystem.

  • Digital India Week

    Organized by the Government of India, Digital India Week is a series of events and initiatives aimed at promoting the Digital India vision. These events focus on driving digital adoption across various sectors, fostering e-governance, and increasing digital literacy.

  • Internet Governance and Cybersecurity Workshops

    Various cybersecurity and governance workshops are organized across India by institutions, non-profits, and government agencies. These events often include training sessions on cybersecurity policies, data protection, digital law, and the evolving landscape of internet governance.

  • UA Day

    UA Day (Universal Acceptance Day) is an important event focused on promoting Universal Acceptance of domain names and email addresses in all scripts and languages. It aims to encourage the use of non-Latin scripts on the internet, fostering inclusivity and diversity in the global digital ecosystem.

  • NIXI Internship Program

    The National Internet Exchange of India (NIXI) offers an internship program that provides hands-on experience in various aspects of internet governance, including DNS management, internet infrastructure, and policy advocacy. This program offers interns valuable exposure to the national and global digital policy landscape.
