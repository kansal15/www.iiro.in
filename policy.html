Policy

At IIRO, we are committed to advancing a secure, inclusive, and accessible digital future through the formulation and dissemination of key internet governance policies. Our focus is on ensuring that the policies we develop and support align with international standards while addressing local needs. Below are some of the essential policy documents and resources:

  • ICANN Internet Governance Policy Documents

    ICANN plays a crucial role in the global internet ecosystem. Their policy documents guide various internet governance aspects such as domain name system (DNS) management, security, and privacy. IIRO supports and advocates for these policies to ensure the global internet remains stable and secure. [ICANN Policy Documents](https://www.icann.org/resources/pages/policy-documents-en)

  • WSIS (World Summit on the Information Society) Policy Documents

    The outcome documents of the World Summit on the Information Society (WSIS) provide essential frameworks on internet governance, digital inclusion, and multi-stakeholder collaboration. IIRO supports the goals outlined by WSIS and continues to work towards achieving them at both national and regional levels. [WSIS Outcome Documents](https://www.itu.int/net/wsis/docs/)

These documents are central to our advocacy work and help shape the future of internet governance at national and global levels. They are essential for understanding how different stakeholders work together to maintain a secure, open, and inclusive digital environment.
