Research

At the India Internet Research Foundation (IIRO), our research aims to foster a better understanding of the evolving internet landscape and its impact on India. We focus on exploring critical areas that drive the digital transformation of our nation, addressing challenges, and identifying opportunities for growth and development in the digital age.

Key Research Areas
  • Internet Governance and Policy

    Our research focuses on the evolving digital policies and their impact on India’s internet ecosystem. We explore regulations governing digital access, privacy, security, and freedom of expression, while offering policy recommendations for a sustainable and inclusive digital future.

  • Digital Infrastructure

    We analyze the state of India’s digital infrastructure, including broadband, 5G, and satellite connectivity, with an emphasis on improving internet access across rural and underserved regions. Our work is dedicated to advancing connectivity and bridging the digital divide.

  • Cybersecurity and Data Protection

    In today’s digital world, security is paramount. IIRO conducts research on emerging cybersecurity threats and data privacy issues, helping to develop frameworks for safer online transactions, digital banking, and overall internet safety across sectors.

  • Emerging Technologies

    Our research delves into the role of emerging technologies like Artificial Intelligence (AI), Blockchain, and the Internet of Things (IoT) in shaping India’s internet future. We explore their potential to drive innovation in sectors such as healthcare, education, and governance.

  • Digital Inclusion

    We are committed to ensuring that the internet is accessible to all. Our studies focus on creating strategies for greater digital inclusion, exploring how to make the internet more accessible to marginalized populations, including the elderly, people with disabilities, and economically disadvantaged communities.

  • E-commerce and the Digital Economy

    India’s digital economy is growing rapidly. IIRO’s research examines the growth of e-commerce, digital payment systems, and other key sectors that are shaping India’s digital economy. We assess regulatory challenges and propose strategies for fostering a thriving digital marketplace.

  • Social Media and Content

    The role of social media in society is one of our primary research focuses. We explore the influence of social platforms on communication, politics, and social movements. Our work also includes examining issues around content regulation, misinformation, and digital ethics.

  • Impact of the Internet on Society

    We study how the widespread use of the internet is transforming society. From education to employment and cultural shifts, IIRO’s research looks at how the internet influences various aspects of life and the challenges that come with this digital transformation.

  • International Collaboration and Research

    In an increasingly interconnected world, IIRO collaborates with global research organizations to stay at the forefront of international internet governance trends. Our research also contributes to global discussions on digital rights, internet freedom, and global policy frameworks.

Join Us in Shaping India’s Digital Future

At IIRO, we are dedicated to conducting research that influences policy, enhances digital inclusion, and drives technological innovation. By exploring these critical areas, we aim to ensure that India’s internet ecosystem remains dynamic, secure, and accessible to all.
